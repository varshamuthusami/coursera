Our Menu

chicken

Chicken is the most common type of poultry in the world. Owing to the relative ease and low cost of raising chickens in comparison to mammals such as cattle or hogs chicken meat and chicken eggs have become prevalent in numerous cuisines.

beef

Beef is the culinary name for meat from cattle. Beef can be prepared in various ways; cuts are often used for steak, which can be cooked to varying degrees of doneness, while trimmings are often ground or minced, as found in most hamburgers. Beef contains protein, iron, and vitamin B12.

sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice, usually with some sugar and salt, a variety of ingredients, such as seafood often raw and vegetables. Styles of sushi and its presentation vary widely, but the one key ingredient is "sushi rice", also referred to as shari, or sumeshi.
